Lentil Bolognese

Description

A hearty vegetarian alternative to the classic bolognese sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 cloves of garlic, pressed
  • 2 carrots, finely diced
  • 2 sticks of celery, finely diced
  • 1 cup red lentils, raw
  • 2 tins chopped tomatoes (400g each)

Recipe

  1. Heat the oil in a large saucepan
  2. Fry the onions until translucent
  3. Add the garlic and fry until fragrant
  4. Throw in the carrots and celery and fry until slightly softened
  5. Stir in the lentils and the chopped tomatoes
  6. Fill one of the tomato tins with water and pour that in
  7. Simmer until the lentils are cooked through, adding more water if needed
  8. Add salt and pepper to taste and serve with spaghetti or the pasta of your choice!
