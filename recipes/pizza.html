Pizza

Description

Beginner friendly pizza recipe. This recipe makes enough dough two pizzas (or one ernormous one).

Ingredients (for 2 pizzas!)

  • 250g plain flour (plus extra for dusting)
  • 8g live yeast
  • 1 cup lukewarm water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of sugar
  • 200g tin of finely chopped tomatoes
  • 400g mozzarella, diced
  • Whatever you want as toppings!

Recipe

  1. Mix the flour and salt in a medium sized mixing bowl
  2. Stir the yeast into the water until dissolved, then stir in the sugar and oil
  3. Add the wet ingredients to the flour and mix until a dough is formed
  4. Cover and leave to rise for at least 2 hours
  5. Preheat the oven to 230C
  6. Divide the dough in half and roll out on a lightly dusted surface to around 33cm across
  7. spread the tomatoes evenly over the 2 pizzas
  8. Add your toppings
  9. Scatter the mozzarella evenly across both pizzas
  10. Bake in the oven until cooked through (around 12 minutes)
