Risotto

Description

A quick and easy vegetable risotto. Feeds around 4, depending on how hungry everyone is!

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp rapeseed oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 2 carrots, peeled and diced
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 2 cups risotto rice
  • 4 cups hot water
  • 50g butter
  • 20g parmesan (plus extra to top)

Recipe

  1. Heat the oil in a large saucepan and fry the onions until translucent
  2. Add the carrots, zucchini and frozen peas, and fry until slightly softened
  3. Add the risotto rice and fry for a few minutes
  4. Throw in some salt and pepper and stir through
  5. Pour in the hot water, stri through and bring to a boil
  6. Turn the heat down and simmer until the rice is cooked through, add more water if necessary
  7. Throw in the parmesan and butter, salt to taste and serve!
